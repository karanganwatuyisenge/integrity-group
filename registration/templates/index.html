About Our Group

Integrity Aquatic Therapy & Orthopedics Centre has the vision of providing establishments set on the principles of competency,commitment,compassion,convenience and efficiency.We believe in being the true 'partners in health' for the community,ensuring that along with guidance,attention and care,expertise is delivered with cutting edge best practice,innovation and precision.

At Integrity Aquatic Therapy & Orthopedics centre,we believe that the journey of success needs forces of passion & dedication.These are imperative pre-requisites for growth and quality performance and we want to evolve as a corporate health facility with a unique and competent strategy.

Our Mission

To Provide the Best Care and Specialized Rehabilitation and Medical Services to the People and Community.We are Committed to Excellence in the Delivery of Rehabilitation and Medical Careina Cost-Effective and Timely Manner.

Our Vision

To become a World-class Leading Aquatic Therapy & Orthopedic Centre in the Country and Region providing Client-centered Care with Evidence-Based Services.
